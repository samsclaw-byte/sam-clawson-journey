## üí° Pro Tips for New Parent Valentine's

1. **Lower expectations** - It's okay if it's not perfect. Being together matters.
2. **Plan B** - Have a backup plan in case baby is fussy/sick.
3. **Gift practicality** - Sophie might appreciate a spa voucher more than flowers right now.
4. **Include baby** - A small "from baby" gift/card melts hearts.
5. **Don't compare** - Your Valentine's won't look like Instagram. That's fine.
6. **Quality over quantity** - 1 hour of genuine connection beats 4 hours of fancy dinner where you're both stressed.
